About the Company

EMV 2 Motors was founded in 2020 by Enrico Miguel N. Salvador, naming it in honor of his father Edsel, his grandfather Mauro, and his granduncles Virgilio and Valentin.

Virgilio died shortly before the company was founded, while Mauro and Valentin were architects. This naming was reflected in the company's motto: "We serve cars, in honor of the people."

The company's first car is the Model 1. Right now, the company boasts 3 cars, which can be viewed above.
