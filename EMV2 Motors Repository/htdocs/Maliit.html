EMV 2 Motors Maliit


EMV 2 Motors Maliit , the small but terrible car, with its lightweight body and aerodynamic design!

First Generation


The original plan of the EMV 2 Motors Maliit (First Generation).

EMV 2 Maliit is the company's first try in making microcars, and has a body made of an entirely new material, carboard, which is lighter than the previous material the company used. It is a four-seater car, together with space at the back.

Second Generation


The original plan of the EMV 2 Motors Maliit (Second Generation).

Originally to be called EMV 2 Munti, it become instead the second generation of the EMV 2 Maliit, as it shares the share body with the first generation model, only with a more aerodynamic design and better equipment.
